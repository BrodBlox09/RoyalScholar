Tutor a Session

How Tutoring Works

  1. Signing Up for Requests

    2. Students request tutoring sessions through the Request a Session form. You'll be notified when a request matches your subject preferences. You can review all open requests from the Tutor a Session page.

  2. Preparing for the Session

    3. Before each session, you'll receive details such as the subject, time, and student information. Please review the relevant material and prepare helpful resources or practice problems to bring to the session.

  3. Where to Go
    • Location: B3 Neighborhood
    • Check-in: Ms. Harris
  4. During the Session

    5. Focus on understanding the student's challenges. Guide them through examples and encourage active participation. Remember: effective tutoring is about collaboration, patience, and clear communication.

  5. Post-Session Reflection (Required)

    6. After each session, complete the Tutor Session Reflection Form to record progress and note areas for improvement. This step is required to receive service hour credit. Reflections help future tutors continue supporting the student effectively.

Thank you for your commitment to helping RHS students succeed. Your professionalism and dedication as a tutor make a meaningful difference every day.

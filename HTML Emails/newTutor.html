How it Works

  1. Book a Session

    2. Go to the Request a Session page and fill out the form. You will be sent a confirmation email with everything you entered in the form.

  2. How to Prepare

    3. Bring any relevant class work and supplies with you to the B3 neighborhood.

  3. Where to Go
    • Location: B3 Neighborhood
    • Check-in with: Ms. Harris
  4. During the Session

    5. Show your tutor what you've been struggling with and they'll try their best to help.

  5. Post-Session

    6. You will be prompted to fill out a post-session reflection form rating your experience with us and letting us know how we might improve.

Request a Session

Your Privacy

Anything and everything you discuss with your tutor is kept confidential; your tutor is not allowed to share what you tell them with anyone, even teachers, except on rare occasions.

Our tutors are trained to stay respectful and understanding, so feel free to share what you are truly struggling with and don't feel embarrased.

Interested in Contributing?

Become a RoyalScholar tutor! Contribute to RHS's student body by tutoring, and gain service hours at the same time. And the best part? You don't need to be part of NHS to join!

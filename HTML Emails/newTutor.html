Tutor a Session

What's Next?

Here's how tutoring with RoyalScholar works:

  1. Signing Up for Requests

    2. Students will request tutoring sessions via the Request a Session form. You'll be notified if the session subject/class is one of the preferences you've selected. See the Tutor a Session page to review upcoming sessions.

  2. Before the Session

    3. You'll be given the subject, class, session time, and relevant information about the student you'll be tutoring that we've collected. To be prepared for the session, we recommend reviewing the subject material and any specific topics the student has mentioned beforehand. Feel free to bring notes and resources that you think might help.

  3. Where to Go
    • Location: B3 Neighborhood
    • Check-in with: Ms. Harris
  4. During the Session

    5. Listen to your student and try your best to help.

  5. Post-Session

    6. You will be required to fill out a post-session reflection form where you can leave notes about the student's progress, helping future tutors support them in the future. Not filling out the required post-session reflection form will result in you recieving no service hours for the session.

  6. Verifying Service Hours

    7. At any time, you can get a list of all of your completed sessions by going to the My Sessions page. Here you can generate and print a RoyalScholar Service Hour Report to verify your service hours.

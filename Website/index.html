FAQ

Is RoyalScholar free to use?

Yes, RoyalScholar is completely free to use.

What is a tutoring session?

A tutoring session is a one-on-one meeting between you and a tutor to help with your studies.

How do I request a session?

You can request a session by clicking on the subject you need help with above, or by going to the Request a Session page.

What subjects are available?

We offer tutoring in English, science, social studies, mathematics, and world languages.

Where and when do sessions take place?

Tutoring sessions happen in the B3 neighborhood during seminar or another time of your choosing.

Can I become a tutor?

Yes! Any student can become a tutor. If you're passionate about helping others grow academically apply here to join our tutor team.

Who do I contact for more questions?

Email us at rhs.royalscholar@dodea.edu or speak with Ms. Harris in B3.
